About Us

St. Xavier's Campus, Andheri, is a prestigious institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Established in 1952, our campus nurtures students to become leaders and innovators across diverse fields. With over 70 years of excellence, we continue to uphold the highest standards in education.
